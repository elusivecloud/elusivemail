A note on writing to us

When you email us you are sending a normal message over the internet, so we and your own provider can see it. Do not put passwords or recovery codes in an email to us. We will never ask for them, and there is nothing we could do with them anyway in end-to-end mode.

We are small, so replies can take a few days. Security reports jump the queue.
