Plain terms

What we can and can’t see.

You should know the exact shape of what you’re handing us. Here it is, both sides.

Sealed to your key
  • Your message bodies and subject lines
  • Your attachments
  • Who a stored message is from and to
  • Your username and aliases, and whether you've read a message
  • In keyfile mode, the key itself. We hold nothing to hand over.

Encrypted to a key only you hold. Your stored mail is noise to us.

Visible to us
  • Who a message is from and to at the moment it passes through. Delivery needs it; the stored copy is sealed.
  • When mail arrives, and to which of your aliases
  • Incoming mail the instant it arrives, before we seal it to your key, except when a sender's own app already encrypted it to you with PGP; that mail reaches us sealed and we never see the plaintext

Delivery needs these, so we handle them. We never write them to a log.

Keyfile mode is the strictest setting. You keep the only copy of your key and we store none of it, so there is nothing to hand over and nothing even we can recover.

Built in the open

What we build as we grow.

Elusive makes no money and answers to no investors. As people join, we commit to shipping and funding these, in order. Here is the plan, in public.

0 inboxes created
The path
  1. 300

    Public API and integrations

    A documented API with tokens you control, so aliases and mail can plug into your own tools and scripts.

  2. 800

    Mobile and desktop apps

    Native apps for mobile and desktop, and donations open to start funding what comes next.

  3. 1,500

    Our own servers

    Funded by those donations, we move onto standalone hardware we run ourselves in Switzerland.

The continuation
  1. 2,500

    Multi-server structure

    Mail, keys and the web app split across separate machines, so no single box ever holds everything.

  2. 4,000

    Independent audit

    We commission a third-party security audit and publish the results in full.

  3. 6,000

    Encrypted communicator

    A private messenger built on the same keys, for when email is not enough.

  4. 10,000

    Full infrastructure

    The whole private suite: encrypted file sharing and drive on the same keys, running on our own hardware.

Some numbers and details here may shift as we learn what things really cost. The commitments themselves stay, and we will do our best to reach every one.
